Topogram

Social network analysis for Humans

An open-source toolkit to process, visualize and analyze networks.

Download Get Started

Release notes

Semantic Analysis

A powerful indexing engine based on allows you to watch evolution of keywords and expressions in your data.

Discussion Dynamics

Online or offline, conversations can not be fitted in a single graph. Topogram shows how users interacts words by analyzing semantics and circulation.

Location-based analysis

Projection on maps with timelines allows to see the geographical spread of the information. Maps can be printed, exported and shared easily

Upload & export data

Upload raw CSV data files and start to use Topogram. Export results on CSV and PNG.

Chinese Social Media

Based on research about the Chinese social web, Topogram is ready-to-use adapter for Sina Weibo and support Chinese text.

Efficient technological stack

Topogram use Python to analyse and Meteor to serve results. The client side uses a visualization engine based on d3.js, cytoscape.js and HTML5.

Python, Meteor, d3.js and more.
